  • Ahri

    Tips & Tricks

    Ahri is amazing in the mid-game with her mobility and pick potential with her R off cooldown

  • Akali

    Tips & Tricks

    Akali is the only energy champion without energy region in her kit, don't just spam her abilities

    Akali is a great duelist. high sustain with Crown or Riftmaker, massive mobility with her E and R, long and safe fights with her W and great burst when you add all that together

    Akali is known for her roaming and dominating squishes, so utilize vision and vision control

  • Akshan

    Tips & Tricks

    Akshan is a very strong lane bully with early game aggression thanks to his E and his innate Passive sheild

    Akshan doesn't do well against heavy cc comps since he relies on getting on the enemy quickly, and doesn't do very well against longer ranged champions who can duel with him

    Akshan's level 2 can win you the lane with the right aggression and trading, you can also easily dive and take an extra tower hit because of your Passive
